Let's make a cake

It's going to be a piece of cake!

Ingredients

  • Strawberry 🍓
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Syrup
  • Milk
  • venila extract

Instructions

  1. 1.Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. 2.In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  3. 3.In another bowl, beat the eggs and then add the milk and vanilla extract.
  4. 4.Combine the wet and dry ingredients, and mix until smooth.
  5. 5.Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.
  6. 6.Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. 7.Let the cake cool before frosting or serving.
